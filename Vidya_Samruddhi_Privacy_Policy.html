Privacy Policy — Required Summary

This policy describes the data collected and how it is used for the Vidya Samruddhi mobile application.

Note: This app is developed and published by an individual developer, Sukesh Nayak, for educational purposes.

1. Data we collect

  • Personal data (collected only if provided): Name, Email — for account creation and authentication.
  • Profile photo: Uploaded or captured by the user via Camera or selected from the device gallery.
  • Device & diagnostic data: Device model, OS version, app usage events, crash logs for analytics and troubleshooting.
  • Biometric: Biometric authentication (fingerprint/biometric) is used only locally via Android Biometric APIs. We do not collect or store raw biometric templates.

2. Purpose of collection

  • Authentication and account management (sign-in, recovery).
  • App functionality (profile management, offline downloads, notifications).
  • Improving app performance and stability (analytics, crash reporting).
  • Customer support and important service messages.

3. Permissions requested

  • USE_BIOMETRIC / USE_FINGERPRINT: Local device authentication only (handled by the OS).
  • CAMERA: Capture profile images.
  • READ_MEDIA_IMAGES: Select profile images from device storage (Android 13+ scoped storage).
  • INTERNET: Server communication, authentication, content delivery.
  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS: Deliver account/app notifications.

4. Third-party services

We use trusted third-party providers (for example: Firebase Analytics, Crashlytics) to provide analytics and crash reporting. These providers receive limited diagnostic data under their own privacy policies. We do not sell personal data to third parties.

5. Data storage, retention & security

Personal data is retained only as long as necessary to provide services and to meet legal obligations. Sensitive authentication data is encrypted in transit and at rest. Biometric verification is performed locally by the device OS and secure hardware; we never transmit raw biometric templates to our servers.

6. Children

The app is intended for educational use. Children under 13 should use the app with parental supervision. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children without parental consent and comply with applicable laws (e.g., COPPA/GDPR where applicable).

7. User rights & deletion requests

Users may request access, correction, or deletion of their personal data. To exercise these rights, contact us (see below). Verified deletion requests will be processed in accordance with applicable law and our retention policies.
